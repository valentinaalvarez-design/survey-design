# survey-design.skill

A survey design coach for Claude. Drop it in and Claude becomes a collaborative research partner. From writing a first survey question to designing a MaxDiff study.
---

## What it does

| If you bring... | Claude will... |
|---|---|
| A vague idea ("I want to ask users about X") | Run a research brief, then design the full survey |
| A research plan doc | Parse it and design questions directly from your goals |
| A draft survey | Review it across strategy, structure, and question quality |
| A method name (MaxDiff, Kano, JTBD...) | Explain it, validate fit, then design it with you |

---

## Trigger phrases

Works when you say things like:

> *"I have a survey that needs work"*
> *"Can you review this?"*
> *"We're running a Kano study"*
> *"Turn my research plan into a survey"*

---

## Methods covered

**Standard**
- Research brief + survey design from scratch
- Research plan parsing (maps background, key questions, and outcomes directly to survey design)
- Question review and rewrite
- Survey structure and flow logic
- Platform-specific guidance: Survicate, SurveyMonkey, Google Forms

**Advanced**
- MaxDiff / best-worst scaling
- Conjoint analysis (CBC)
- JTBD-framed surveys
- Kano model surveys
- Concept testing surveys

---

## Designed for

PMs and designers who need research-quality output without a researcher in the room — and researchers who want a faster drafting partner for everything from intercepts to conjoint studies.
